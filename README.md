# sauder-teaching-wordpress-template

A demo is hosted here: https://blogs.ubc.ca/sauder2026ss/   


## Instructions
1. Create your blog in blogs.ubc.ca. If you have been given a naming convention for your site by Learning Services, please follow that.
1. Go to ''Plugins'' and enable the following plugins. (Note: some may already be active.)
1. 1. Accordion Blocks
1. 1. Accordion Shortcode
1. 1. Add Users Sidebar Widget
1. 1. Content Visibility
1. 1. Content Visibility Date and Time
1. 1. Content Visibility User Role
1. 1. Display Posts
1. 1. Gutenberg
1. 1. List Authors
1. 1. Simpler CSS
1. 1. The Icon Block
1. 1. Unfiltered MU
1. 1. WP Render Blogroll Links
1. Go to ''Appearance'', enabling the ''Twenty Twenty'' theme.
1. To set the theme overrides:
1. 1. Select all and copy the following to clipboard: https://raw.githubusercontent.com/saud-learning-services/sauder-teaching-wordpress-template/refs/heads/main/simple-css-copy-and-paste.css
1. 1. Go to ''Appearance > Custom CSS'' and paste the content into the text field there.
1. 1. Save the Simple CSS settings.
